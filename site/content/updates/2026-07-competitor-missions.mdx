Recent developments in Venus cloud exploration:

- **Rocket Lab Venus Life Finder**: Private mission planning 2026 launch. Focused on autofluorescence nephelometer to detect organic compounds in clouds at ~50km altitude. Single instrument descent probe.

- **NASA Aerobot concepts**: Super-pressure variable altitude balloon cycling 48-60km. 30+ day missions for habitability characterization. Proposed for ~2026-2028 windows.

- **ESA Venus Entry Probe (VEP)**: Aerobot + orbiter for middle cloud layer analysis. Historical concept with modern relevance.

These missions validate the scientific interest in the cloud layer and de-risk aspects of AETHER's approach. AETHER remains unique in targeting long-duration float with broad payload.
