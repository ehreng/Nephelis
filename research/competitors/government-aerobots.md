# Government / agency aerobot concepts

**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-08

## NASA Venus aerobot concepts

Multiple NASA studies have examined variable-altitude balloons and aerobots for the 48–60 km range. These inform materials, inflation, and navigation risk for Cloudseeker even when full missions are unfunded or delayed.

## ESA / other

- Historical: Venus Express (orbital atmosphere)
- Conceptual: VEP-class balloon ideas periodically resurface in white papers

## Heritage that matters operationally

- **VEGA 1 & 2 (1985):** only successful Venus balloon flights — gold standard for duration/float lessons
- **Pioneer Venus multiprobe:** atmospheric structure entry profiles
