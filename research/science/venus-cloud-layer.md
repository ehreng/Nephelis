# Venus cloud layer — science notes

**Status:** living document · update via weekly research loop  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-08

## Habitable zone altitudes

- Roughly **48–60 km** altitude spans Earth-like temperature and pressure bands.
- Project AETHER targets ~**55 km** for super-pressure float operations.
- Surface conditions remain extreme; the *cloud deck* is the habitation-relevant layer.

## Key physical parameters (order of magnitude)

| Parameter | ~55 km Venus | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Pressure | ~0.5–1 bar | Near Earth surface |
| Temperature | ~0–50 °C | Altitude-dependent |
| Atmosphere | ~96.5% CO₂, ~3.5% N₂ | ISRU feedstock |
| Solar flux | High above clouds | Power opportunity |
| Hazards | H₂SO₄ aerosols | Materials / sealing |
