# Partner Outreach Prompt

Use `site/content/data/partners.json` and `decks/press-kit.md`.

For each **P0/P1** pipeline entry with status `research` or `prospect`:

1. Draft a short outreach email (≤120 words) using press-kit boilerplate.
2. List the concrete ask from the `ask` field.
3. Suggest a 7-day next_step update for the JSON after send.

Do not invent organization names that are not in the pipeline. Prefer "university atmospheric group" over fake entities until a real target is named by a human.
